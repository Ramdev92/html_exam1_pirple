Chicken Dry Curry Recipe Time

Ingredients Needed To Cook Chicken Curry:

  • Chicken 1/2 kg - Sliced into small pieces
  • Onions - 2 or 3 sliced into small pieces
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Sliced Green Chillies
  • Oil - 5 teaspoon
  • Spices - 2 teaspoon
  • Salt - 2 teaspoon
  • Mirchi Powder - 2 teaspoon
  • Coriander

Steps to Prepare the Dish

  1. Take a pan. Heat it in medium flame and pour oil.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pan, continue heating in medium flame for 10 mins.
  3. Add garlic paste.
  4. After 5 mins, Drop the sliced green chillies, sliced onions, salt and mirchi powder into pan, mix well.
  5. Close the lid, and let the chicken boil for 10 mins.
  6. Now add spices and stir continously.
  7. Check the piece boil status and let it fry until you see light golden color.
  8. After 5 mins, top it with coriander leaves and mix well. You can now serve this yummy dish.
